Welcome to our site

By Martin Ribelotta | August 6, 2020

Hello everyone, welcome to the engineering blog “ourEmbedded”.

We hope this blog section will be useful for all of you. We will be publishing technical notes, works carried out by our engineers, and comments on new techniques and products that we consider relevant.

Additionally, everyone is welcome to send questions, doubts or suggestions to our engineering staff.

About ourEmbeddeds

We are a fresh company composed by entusiast engineers and experts with great skills in electronic engineering, deep embedded software, communication systems, RF technologies and many other areas of interest.

Our expectations are to be a “value adder” for your business and ventures … We consider ourselves as a enablers for high complexity engineering ventures, and are ready to help you and your team take your products to the next level.

Values and skills

Beyond the technical skills, we have a vision of entrepreneurship:

  • All problems have a elegant solution
  • All solutions must be clean and suitable to be extended
  • Every problem is a challenge and every challenge is an opportunity to explore new possibilities
  • Excellence has real value
  • Engineering is a creative discovery process
  • We will accompany you in the search for the solution to your engineering problems. It is more fun than you can imagine!
